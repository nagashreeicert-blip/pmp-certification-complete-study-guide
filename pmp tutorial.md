## Tutorial 1: What is PMP Certification?
PMP stands for **Project Management Professional**.  
It is a globally recognized certification by PMI (Project Management Institute).  

**Why PMP is important:**  
- Recognized worldwide  
- Increases salary and promotions  
- Shows you know professional project management skills  
- Helps you manage projects efficiently  

---

## Tutorial 2: Understanding Project Management Basics
### Key Terms:
- **Project:** Temporary work with start, end, and a goal.  
- **Project Manager:** The person who plans, executes, and closes the project.  
- **Scope:** Total work required to complete a project.  
- **Stakeholder:** Anyone affected by the project.  
- **Risk:** Something that may impact the project positively or negatively.  

---

## Tutorial 3: Project Lifecycle
Every project has 5 phases:

1. **Initiation:** Define the project, create Project Charter.  
2. **Planning:** Make a detailed plan (schedule, budget, resources).  
3. **Execution:** Do the work according to the plan.  
4. **Monitoring & Controlling:** Track progress, manage risks, and solve issues.  
5. **Closure:** Complete the project and get approval.

## Tutorial 5: Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)
**WBS** is a tool to divide a project into smaller, manageable tasks.  
**Steps to create WBS:**  
1. Identify main deliverables.  
2. Break them into smaller tasks.  
3. Assign tasks to team members.
